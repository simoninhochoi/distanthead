## US-China Relations Seminar

A monthly seminar exploring how middle powers can move beyond the role of passive bystanders in US-China relations and actively steer the superpower rivalry toward outcomes that serve the broader global order.

## Circle of the Fish

An working group on studies of East Asian international order in the past and present

## Research Group on Korean Diplomatic History

A monthly seminar group on global diplomatic history of Korean peninsular from the 5th century to the 19th century

## AI and Future of the Earth

A philosophical and political inquiry into how the rise of artificial intelligence is transforming the foundations of global order. As human and machine cognition become increasingly entangled, this seminar examines what the emergence of hybrid intelligence means for two pillars of the modern world: the sovereign state system built on human political agency, and the planetary order premised on humanity's unique stewardship of the Earth.
