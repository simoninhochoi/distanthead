## US-China Relations Seminar

A monthly seminar exploring how middle powers can move beyond passive bystander roles in US-China rivalry to actively shape outcomes serving the broader global order.

## Circle of the Fish

A working group on East Asian international order, past and present.

## Research Group on Korean Diplomatic History

A monthly seminar on global diplomatic history of the Korean peninsula from the 5th to 19th century.

## AI and Future of the Earth

A philosophical and political inquiry into how AI is transforming the foundations of global order. Examines what hybrid intelligence means for the sovereign state system and planetary stewardship premised on humanity's unique role.
